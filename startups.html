Early-Stage Concepts

Future Ideas Aligned with RectifyGo

Exploratory concepts that extend RectifyGo's decarbonization mission into adjacent industries. These are early-stage ideas currently in conceptual development.

HydroVolt

An early-stage concept exploring modular hydrogen fuel cell systems designed to provide zero-carbon backup power for data centers and cloud infrastructure. The idea envisions scalable, containerized power blocks that could integrate with renewable energy grids while ensuring continuous uptime for mission-critical computing facilities.

HyBlend

An exploratory concept for a digital platform that would track and certify clean hydrogen throughout the supply chain for aviation and heavy transportation. The envisioned system would provide end-to-end provenance tracking, connect hydrogen production to consumption data, and help operators meet emerging regulatory requirements for sustainable fuel reporting.
